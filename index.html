About the lab

关于实验室

      智能化系统实验室(Intelligent System Laboratory，ISL)隶属于青海大学化工机械系。 我们的研究领域包括大规模网络化系统（复杂网络结构的基本特性分析），生物系统辨识（生物信息处理、参数辨识、结构辨识与状态估计），人工智能（机器学习、深度学习、度量学习、哈希学习、强化学习和多方面的学习）、智能控制与分析（光伏储能系统优化控制、电力网络化系统优化控制），模式识别(指纹识别、掌纹识别、人脸识别和步态识别)，计算机视觉(计算机视觉目标检测、跟踪、识别和场景理解)。

Intelligent System Laboratory (ISL) is affiliated with the Department of Chemical Machinery, Qinghai University. Our research areas include large-scale networked systems (analysis of basic characteristics for complex network structures), biological system identification and information processing (biological information processing, parameter identification, structure identification and state estimation), Artificial intelligence (machine learning, deep learning, metric learning, hash learning, reinforcement learning and multifaceted learning), intelligent control and analysis (optimal control of photovoltaic energy storage system, optimal control of power network system), pattern recognition (fingerprint recognition, palmprint recognition, face recognition and gait recognition), computer vision (computer vision object detection, tracking, recognition and scene understanding).
